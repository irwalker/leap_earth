Leap Explorer

Leap Motion globe explorer. Navigate with one hand. To spin the globe horizontally, move your hand left/right. To move the globe up and down, move you hand forward or backward. Zoom is controlled by moving the hand up or down. Finer grained zoom control is given using gesture control, by moving the finger in a circlular movement. Zoom in by moving your finger clockwise, out by moving your finger anti-clockwise.
The leap motion hand box will display what the leap motion senses. If control is lost, move your hand outside the range and all movement on page will cease.
